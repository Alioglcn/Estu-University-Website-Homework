INSTITUES

# Graduate Education Institute Institute of Transportation Sciences Earth and Space Sciences Institute
1 Established with the Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette dated May 18, 2018 and numbered 30425, Graduate Education Institute affiliated to Eskişehir Technical University was established by the Presidential decision numbered 967 published in the Official Gazette dated April 18, 2019 and numbered 30749. It started its educational activities in the second semester. The Transportation Economics Research Institute was established within Anadolu University in 1993 with the Decree Law No. 496, affiliated to the Rectorate. Changing the name of the Transportation Economics Research Institute to “Transportation Sciences Institute” was decided by the Council of Ministers on 4/10/2012 and entered into force after being published in the Official Gazette dated 02 November 2012 and numbered 28455. The Institute of Transportation Sciences is the first and only institute in its field. The main purpose of our institute is to obtain the necessary information and analyzes for the discovery of human settlements that do not threaten nature and are not threatened by nature. The studies on Remote Sensing and Geographic Information Systems, which started in 1989 under the Computer Research and Application Center of Anadolu University, continued within the Satellite and Space Sciences Research Institute, which was officially established in 1993. It has been changed to Earth and Space Sciences Institute since November 2012.
