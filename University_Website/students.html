Important information for the students


For Turkish Students;

The first stage of the exam, which consists of two stages, is the Basic Proficiency Test (TYT), and the second stage is the Field Proficiency Test (AYT). Candidates who exceed 180 points can participate in AYT. AYT; It consists of Turkish, Basic Mathematics, Social Sciences and Science tests. Candidates who get 180 points out of 500 points in AYT can choose undergraduate programs. Candidates are placed in undergraduate programs via computer by ÖSYM, based on the scores they get from TYT and AYT, and their secondary education (high school in the Turkish Education System) achievement scores.


For Foreign Students;

The "Foreign Student Examination (YÖS)" conducted by the Assessment, Selection and Placement Center (ÖSYM) for foreign students has been abolished since the 2010-2011 academic year. Instead of this exam, in Eskişehir Technical University, in line with the principles determined by the Council of Higher Education, the result of the "Higher Education Transfer and Placement Exam for Associate/Undergraduate Students to be Admitted to Anadolu University from Abroad (ANADOLUYÖS)" and SAT 1, GCE, IB, ABITUR etc. according to the scores; of foreign nationals, T.R. or TRNC nationals who have completed their entire high school education in a foreign country excluding the TRNC, their applications are evaluated and placed in the previously determined quotas in line with their preferences.
